## Why "Sloppy"?

The name embraces the philosophy that **done is better than perfect**. We want to lower the barrier to creating documentation:

- Don't let perfectionism stop you from starting
- Iterate and improve over time
- Focus on content first, polish later
- "Sloppy" today becomes refined tomorrow
